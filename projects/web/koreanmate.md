Learn Korean naturally through stories!   
KoreanMate is a story-based Korean learning platform designed for A1–B1 learners.  
Practice pronunciation, intonation, and natural rhythm of Korean with our guided lessons.  
Explore our original “Korean Classic Reader” series and discover beautiful stories that help you speak like a native.  

스토리로 한국어를 배우는 플랫폼, KoreanMate.  
발음과 억양 중심의 콘텐츠로 자연스럽게 말하는 한국어를 만들어가요.  

### 기술 스택
TypeScript, MongoDB, React, NestJS, Stripe, Paypal  

### 실행 환경
Web Desktop, Mobile  

### 담당 작업

* 웹 프론트엔드 개발
* 어드민 툴 개발
